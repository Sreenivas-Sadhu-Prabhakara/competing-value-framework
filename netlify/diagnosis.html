// CLOUDVIEW CASE
Overview Diagnosis Strategy Transformation Playbook

Organizational Diagnosis

Mapping the crisis against the Competing Values Framework (CVF).

Collaborate (Clan)

Broken Trust

Previous retrenchments caused redundancy and fear. The culture had lost its "passionate, energetic" entrepreneurial spirit.

Create (Adhocracy)

Technological Stagnation

Rampant illegal tapping of the analogue network degraded signals for legitimate customers. The company lacked a vision for the future.

Control (Hierarchy)

Lack of Discipline

No one could quantify the bleeding. Management had not articulated the magnitude of the problem.

Compete (Market)

Market Vulnerability

Programming costs were 50% of the structure vs the 33% industry standard. High churn due to poor signals.
