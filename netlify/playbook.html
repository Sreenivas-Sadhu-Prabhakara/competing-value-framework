The Leader's Playbook

Key takeaways for modern leadership based on Kotter's "Dual Operating System".

1. Create Urgency

Cruz raised the red flag, quantifying that the company had only 12-18 months left. Without urgency, transformation fails.

2. The Guiding Coalition

He brought in a new leadership team aligned with the new values. Kotter suggests a "volunteer army" from all levels.

3. Quick Wins

The successful negotiation with channels and the immediate impact of the digiboxes provided the "short-term wins" needed to sustain momentum.

4. Institutionalize

Value summaries and "Galing" stories made the change part of the culture. As Cruz noted, "Culture beats process".
