The Turnaround Plan

Jose Cruz implemented a plan to stabilize operations while shifting culture.

INITIATIVE 1

Cost Reduction

Address high programming costs. Cruz used Radical Transparency, showing audited financial statements to partners.

Result: Restored trust led to 35-40% rate cuts.

INITIATIVE 2

Union Cooperation

Met with the union to avoid retrenchment targets. When targets were missed, he gained support for a final retrenchment through honesty.

INITIATIVE 3

Debt Restructuring

Offered creditors 3 options: Debt-to-equity, haircut (reduced payment), or lower interest rates (1-2%).

Cultural Intervention: "Galing"

To fix the "Customer Focus" value, they coined the term "Galing" (Excellence). They held monthly storytelling sessions where employees shared examples of "malasakit" (care).

  • Winners were chauffeured to work and given special chairs.
  • Stories included technicians buying meals for elderly customers.
