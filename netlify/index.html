CRITICAL SITUATION

"Culture Beats Process"

Cloudview, Inc. is a cable television provider featured in a leadership case study regarding organizational turnaround. By 2005, the company was in a critical state, losing nearly half a billion pesos annually due to rampant signal theft and high programming costs.

Under COO Jose Cruz, Cloudview executed a successful turnaround by renegotiating with creditors and launching a bold strategy to digitize its network. This digital leap eliminated illegal connections and allowed for flexible pricing models. These moves, combined with a cultural shift toward values like customer focus, restored the company's financial health and returned it to a growth path.

The Leadership Philosophy

"One of the things I remember learning in school was that culture beats process. If people really believe and are guided by a set of values, those values are likely to be determinant of how they will behave than a set of processes encapsulated in a manual." — Jose Cruz
